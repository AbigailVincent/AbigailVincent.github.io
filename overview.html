StarDew Valley:

Stardew valley is a popular farming game developed by The Secret Police Limited and ConcerenedApe. The game is avaliable avaliable on many platforms including but not limited to: Nintendo Switch, macOS ,PlayStation, etc. You are a farmer in the game that was given a run-down farm in the town:Pelican Town. Players can farm and grow crops, raise animals, mine, fish, and craft. An important element is growing. relationships with towns people: giving gifts, helping with tasks, and attending festivals. Under the crop guide it will provide which crops you can grow in each season. If you grow them in another season, they will not grow. The game is love by many for being a relaxing indie styled game.
