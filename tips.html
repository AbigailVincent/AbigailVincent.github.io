StarDew Valley Tips

  • When planting your crops format it so you can add sprinklers later, it's a must!
  • Save all your resources, many towns people give you taks that require many odd items, keep them in chests!
  • Give gifts based on guide and talk to other towns people, they can send you gifts!
  • When mining if you're losing health leave the mine, if the monsters knock you out, you will lose random items!
  • Make sure your animals are happy by visitng marget to get supplied, she will also offer advice to making your animals like you more.
  • You have to be in bed by 2 AM if youre out of bed, it'll knock you out.
  • Sell items in the box next to your mailbox to level up.
